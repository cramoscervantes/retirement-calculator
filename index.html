Your retirement balance converted back to today's purchasing power, accounting for inflation.

The maximum you can withdrawal monthly without running out of money before your life expectancy.

Your estimated monthly spending need at retirement, adjusted for inflation.

The difference between what you can withdraw and what you need each month. Positive is good.

A quick summary of whether your savings are on track to cover your retirement needs.
